DCIC Research

The name of the reseach was Japanese-American WWII Camps.The goal of the research was to create a names registry and thesaurus of all the names of the Japanese Americans that where in interment camps during WWII. What we had to do was connect two datasets that had information about individuals. Issues we faced were that a lot of the data was not complete, in addition to trying to connect two data sets. The first dataset from the start of the internment camps and the second dataset is that of when they were released.
